Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At Tasquery, we take your data privacy seriously. Our goal is to provide a "frictionless and secure" experience for turning unstructured text into structured tasks.

1. Data Handling & AI Processing

When you use the Tasquery tool (the AI Magic wand), your input text is sent via an encrypted connection to the OpenAI API for processing.

  • No Storage: We do NOT store, log, or cache any of your input text or generated output on our servers. Processing happens in-memory and is discarded immediately after.
  • No AI Training: We use the OpenAI API (Enterprise/Developer tier). According to our provider's policy, data sent through the API is not used to train their global AI models.

2. Waitlist & Email Collection

If you choose to join our waitlist, we store your email address securely (using Google Sheets/Make.com) for:

  • Notifying you about Tasquery updates or product availability.
  • Inviting you to early access or feedback sessions.

We do not share or sell your email to third parties. You can opt-out at any time by contacting us.

3. Security & Confidentiality

While we process data securely, we recommend redacting highly sensitive personal information (like passwords or specific private identifiers) before processing, as a best practice for any AI-based tool.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Tasquery currently does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We value your anonymity. If we implement analytics in the future to improve the product, we will update this policy and provide an opt-out.

5. Contact & Data Deletion

If you have any questions or would like to have your email removed from our waitlist, please reach out to: tasquery@gmail.com.
