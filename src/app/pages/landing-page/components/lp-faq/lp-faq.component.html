FAQ

Do you store my company's data?

No. Tasquery is stateless by default. Unless you create an account and explicitly opt-in to save your history, we do not store your text or generated tickets on our servers.

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can use the core bridge completely for free without logging in. We only require a free account for power users who need longer character limits (up to 2000 chars) and advanced Jira/Slack formatting.

Does it work with Linear or Trello?

Yes. The generated output is heavily structured Markdown, which pastes perfectly into Jira, Linear, GitHub Issues, and most modern project management tools.
