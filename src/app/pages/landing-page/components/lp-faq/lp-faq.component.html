FAQ

Is my data stored?

No. Tasquery processes your input in-memory only. We have a strict no-retention policy and we do not train AI models on your inputs.

Is this free?

Yes – the MVP is completely free to use.

What formats are supported?

Currently, we provide high-quality drafts for Jira, Linear, Trello, and standard Markdown. More integrations are coming to the waitlist members.
