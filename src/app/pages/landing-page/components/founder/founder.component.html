BEHIND TASQUERY

No faceless corporation here.

I built Tasquery because I was tired of the "documentation tax". Spending hours translating my messy notes and Slack threads into structured Jira tickets was killing my productivity. I wanted a tool that does the heavy lifting instantly, without compromising on security.

Tasquery is 100% stateless by design. Your company's data belongs to you, not to an AI's training set. Just paste, generate, and get back to building.
